About Me

Currently completed a six month intensive program at the Nashville Software School. The curriculum is JavaScript & AngularJS for the front-end and Python & Django for the backend. Throughout my life, structured and creative problem-solving projects have always been a passion for me. When I was (much) younger, I discovered BASIC on the Commodore 64. I was excited by a realm of creative possibilities. My philosophy towards programming is my emphasis on computer science and practical training. I believe possessing skills from these two disciplines are the necessary components for a software engineer.

In my previous position in accounting, I found joy in automating solutions for the work I did and creating value there. Last October, I finally took the plunge and left my previous position for a lifelong career I would be passionate about: software engineering. I enjoy using programming as an outlet to explore data, to build products and solve problems. When I am not programming, I am either exercising my body or mind, through indoor climbing or playing jazz and classical piano.
